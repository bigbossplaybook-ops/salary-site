GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE — SITEMAP SUBMISSION INSTRUCTIONS
Boss Playbook Salary Site
=========================================================

STEP 1: Go to Google Search Console
  URL: https://search.google.com/search-console

STEP 2: Add Property
  - Click "Add property" (top left dropdown)
  - Choose "URL prefix" type
  - Enter: https://salary-site-49i.pages.dev
  - Click Continue

STEP 3: Verify Ownership (HTML File Method)
  - Choose "HTML file" verification method
  - Download the verification file (e.g., google1234abcd.html)
  - Place the file in: salary-site/public/
  - Commit and push to main — Cloudflare will auto-deploy
  - Once deployed, click "Verify" in Search Console

STEP 4: Submit the Sitemap
  - In the left sidebar, click "Sitemaps"
  - In the "Add a new sitemap" field, enter:
      https://salary-site-49i.pages.dev/sitemap.xml
  - Click "Submit"

STEP 5: Monitor Indexing
  - Check back in 24-48 hours for indexing status
  - "Coverage" report will show indexed vs. excluded pages
  - ~2,660 pages should be submitted

NOTES:
  - The sitemap is auto-generated at build time via src/pages/sitemap.xml.ts
  - Every push to main triggers a new Cloudflare Pages deploy
  - sitemap.xml.ts includes both legacy occupation pages AND exec salary pages
